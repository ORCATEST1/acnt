FAQ

Common Questions

What does "research use only" mean? +

Our peptides are sold exclusively for in vitro laboratory research. They are not approved for human or veterinary administration, and are not intended for diagnostic use. Purchasers are responsible for compliance with all applicable laws.

How are purity levels measured? +

Purity is determined by reverse-phase HPLC using a C18 column with UV detection at 214 nm. Results are expressed as area percentage. Molecular identity is independently confirmed by mass spectrometry (ESI-MS or MALDI-TOF).

How should peptides be stored? +

Lyophilized peptides should be stored at −20 °C in a dry, dark environment. Once reconstituted in BAC water, store at 2–8 °C and use within 28 days. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles. See each product's COA for specific conditions.

Do you ship internationally? +

We currently ship within the United States via cold chain express shipping. International shipping availability varies by destination. Contact us at orders@ascntlabs.com to confirm your location before ordering.

Can I request a custom batch or concentration? +

Yes. We accommodate custom quantities and concentrations for institutional and bulk research orders. Reach out to research@ascntlabs.com with your specifications and we'll provide a quote within 48 hours.
